We primarily outsource Manpower on a skill requirement basis or project completion basis for a limited period of time. Staffing may be desired when there is a dynamic requirement of people, or there is a requirement of skilled staff, or the requirement is for a limited period.
  • The Staffing services offered by ANJS Tech can address your special and seasonal demands and help minimize the cost involved in hiring permanent employees.
  • Enhance your existing resources and capabilities.
  • Meet aggressive business deadlines.
  • Maintain your company’s competitive edge.
Temporary staffing is an area which helps employers to make an efficient large scale strategic savings on staffing requirements, keep a flexible effective workforce to hand and maintain staff through difficult times. Our Team at ANJS Tech, will place temporary staff according to contracts available. This means employers will not have to outlay costs such as holiday pay, sickness benefits or other fringe benefits of permanent employment - except where official government regulations stipulate temporary staff receive the same benefits.
These savings on temporary staffing - including Statutory Benefit schemes and insurances and perks, can be large when a percentage of the labour force is temporary. Buying in the right skills for the right projects seems like a good idea so far.
